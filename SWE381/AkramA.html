About me

I am a passionate Arabic teacher on Lingo. I can tailor a wide variety of packages according to your needs, starting with foundation Arabic courses, passing through Arabic for specific purposes (Arabic for media, business, and academic purposes), and all the way to Qur’an recitation. To assist you in achieving your goals I will call on my experience as an educator in both universities and public schools.



Work Experience

I have expertise in the following areas:
- Modern standard Arabic (MSA).
- Conversational Arabic.
- Spoken Arabic of Cairo (SAC).
- Arabic For specific purposes (Academic writing, media and business).
- Arabic for children.
- Qur’an recitation.
- Understanding Hadieth.
- Islamic Knowledge for non-Arabs.



Education

I have been armed with a number of important qualifications in the field of education, such as a masters degree in education from Ain Shams University in Cairo, CELTA, and a functional certificate in teaching Arabic to speakers of other languages from the American University in Cairo CCTAFL. In addition, I am a graduate of Al-Azhar University, Faculty of Education- Department of English, not to mention that I am a native speaker of Arabic, so I can certainly help you to improve.
